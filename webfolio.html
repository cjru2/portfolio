ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Carla! I work as a full-stack software developer and am currently based in Metro Manila. My passion lies in crafting functional, clean, and user-friendly websites. Growing up in the Philippines during the internet's early days deeply influenced me. The burgeoning technology scene captured my interest, but what truly inspired me to pursue this career was also a woman in my family—the first developer among us. As a curious kid, watching her engrossed in front of a computer, I'd wonder, "How does this even work?" I yearned to understand and create something similar. Years later, I earned my bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I'm incredibly grateful for the opportunity to learn, grow, and, most importantly, adapt.

My experience spans across various programming languages, scripts, frameworks, and databases, including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, NodeJS, PHP, ASP.NET, C#, VueJS, SQL Server, MongoDB, ExpressJS, among others.

When I'm not immersed in coding, you'll likely find me comfortably clad in my pajamas, engrossed in a good book or indulging in early 2000s romcom movies. Music also holds a special place in my life. Additionally, I have a fondness for writing journal, poetry and songs, though they often remain unfinished, tucked away in the drafts of my note app! :P
